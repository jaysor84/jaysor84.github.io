‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data stored and protected? ‚ñº
All your personal information is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through iCloud.
Can I sync my profile across multiple devices? ‚ñº
Yes! Phone Brain supports iCloud sync, which means your profile information will automatically sync across all your iOS devices signed in with the same Apple ID.
What happens if I delete the app? ‚ñº
If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed from your device. However, if you have iCloud sync enabled, your data will be preserved in iCloud and restored when you reinstall the app.
Can I export my profile information? ‚ñº
Currently, Phone Brain doesn't have a built-in export feature. However, you can take screenshots of your profile or manually copy the information. We're considering adding export functionality in future updates.
How do I update my emergency contact information? ‚ñº
To update your emergency contact information, go to the "Me" tab, tap "Edit" in the top-right corner, scroll down to the Emergency Contact section, and update the relevant fields. Don't forget to tap "Done" to save your changes.

üîí Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Phone Brain ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Phone Brain collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Personal information (name, date of birth, address, phone number)
  • Emergency contact details
  • Medical condition information
  • Profile photos
  • Information about family members, friends, pets, and places

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Displaying your personal profile within the app
  • Helping you remember important information about people and places
  • Providing emergency contact information when needed
  • Syncing your data across your devices (if iCloud sync is enabled)

Data Storage and Security

All your personal information is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your personal information, and it is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data will be securely stored in your personal iCloud account, which is protected by Apple's security measures and your Apple ID credentials.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties. Your data remains private and under your control at all times.

Camera and Photo Library Access

Phone Brain may request access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to add profile photos. These permissions are used solely for this purpose, and photos are stored locally within the app.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the app. If you have iCloud sync enabled, your data will be retained in your iCloud account according to Apple's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

Phone Brain is designed for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
