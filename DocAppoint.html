Instructions

Navigating the App

The app is organized into three main tabs for easy access:

  • Upcoming: View and manage all your future appointments. You can filter them by category using the filter icon in the top-left corner.
  • History: A complete record of all your past appointments, sorted by the most recent.
  • Information: Find app-related info, rate us on the App Store, and discover our other apps.

Adding an Appointment

Tap the "+" icon in the top-right corner of the "Upcoming" screen. Fill in the details for your appointment, including category, doctor, date, and any notes or contact information. Tap "Save" to add it to your list.

Editing or Deleting

To edit an appointment, tap on it from the list to view its details, then tap the "Edit" button. To delete, simply swipe left on an appointment in the list and tap "Delete".

Frequently Asked Questions

How do notifications work?

You can set a custom reminder for each appointment (e.g., 1 hour before, 1 day before). The app will schedule a notification to remind you at your chosen time. Please ensure you have granted notification permissions for the app in your device's settings.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your appointment data is stored securely on your device and is never uploaded to any server. It is completely private to you. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I add a photo to an appointment?

From the appointment's detail screen, tap the paperclip icon in the toolbar. This will allow you to select a photo from your library to attach to the appointment. You can view or delete attachments from the same detail screen.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23 July 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

DocAppoint is designed with your privacy at its core. We believe that your personal health information is yours and yours alone. This policy outlines our data handling practices, which are designed to be as minimal and transparent as possible.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All information you enter into DocAppoint—including appointment details, notes, contacts, and attached photos—is stored exclusively on your local device. We do not have access to it.

Permissions

DocAppoint may request the following permissions to enable key features:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders for your upcoming appointments, as configured by you.
  • Photo Library: To allow you to choose and attach images to your appointments. This access is only used when you initiate it.

Third-Party Links

The app contains links to external services, such as the App Store and Apple Maps. Tapping these links will take you outside of our app. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, deleting the DocAppoint app from your device will permanently delete all of its associated data.
