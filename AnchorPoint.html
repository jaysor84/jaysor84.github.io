How to Use AnchorPoint

Welcome to AnchorPoint! This comprehensive guide will help you make the most of your personal memory anchor app.

üè† Getting Started

1 Download & Launch: After downloading AnchorPoint from the App Store, open the app. You'll see a beautiful launch screen followed by the main dashboard.
2 Explore the Tabs: AnchorPoint has four main sections accessible via the bottom tab bar:
  • Journal: Create different types of journal entries
  • Timeline: View and filter your entries chronologically
  • Appointments: Manage medical and therapy appointments
  • Information: Access app settings and privacy policy

üìù Using the Journal

3 Creating Entries: Tap any category button on the main screen to create a new journal entry:
  • Memory Loss: Record instances of memory difficulties
  • Doctor Appointment: Log medical visits and outcomes
  • Mood Log: Track your emotional well-being
  • Medication Issue: Note medication problems or changes
  • Sleep Issue: Record sleep disturbances
  • Exercise: Log physical activities
  • Sickness: Track illness symptoms
  • Other: Any other important notes
4 Adding Photos: For any journal entry, you can:
  • Select a photo from your library using "Add Photo"
  • Take a new photo using "Take Photo"
  • Remove photos by tapping "Remove Photo"
5 Voice Notes: Use the microphone button to dictate your notes instead of typing. The app will convert your speech to text automatically.

üìÖ Managing Appointments

6 Adding Appointments: Go to the Appointments tab and tap the "+" button:
  • Enter the provider's name
  • Select appointment type (Doctor, Physiotherapist, Occupational Therapist, etc.)
  • Choose date and time
  • Add notes if needed
7 Setting Notifications: Enable reminders for your appointments:
  • Morning of appointment: Get notified at 9 AM on the day
  • 3 days before: Early reminder to prepare
  • 1 week before: Advance notice for planning
8 Managing Appointments:
  • Tap "Edit" to modify appointment details
  • Swipe left on any appointment to delete it
  • View upcoming and past appointments in separate sections

9 Viewing Entries: The Timeline shows all your journal entries in chronological order. Use the filters to:
  • Set date ranges to view specific periods

10 Editing & Deleting:
  • Swipe left on any entry to edit or delete it

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AnchorPoint and who is it for?

AnchorPoint is a personal journaling and appointment management app designed specifically for individuals with dementia, memory issues, or their caregivers. It helps track daily experiences, mood changes, medical appointments, and other important life events in a simple, secure way.

Is my personal data safe and private?

Absolutely! AnchorPoint stores all your data locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your journal entries, photos, or any personal information. Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export and share it yourself.

How do I set up appointment notifications?

When creating or editing an appointment, you'll see three notification options:

  • Morning of appointment: Notifies you at 9:00 AM on the day of your appointment
  • 3 days before: Gives you a 3-day advance notice
  • 1 week before: Provides a full week's notice for planning

Simply toggle on the notifications you want. The app will ask for notification permissions when you first enable this feature.

Can I add photos to my journal entries?

Yes! You can add photos to any journal entry in two ways:

  • Choose from library: Select existing photos from your device
  • Take new photo: Use your device's camera to capture new images

Photos are stored securely with your journal entries and can help provide visual context for your memories and experiences.

How does the speech-to-text feature work?

The speech-to-text feature uses Apple's built-in speech recognition. When creating a journal entry:

  1. Tap the microphone button in the notes section
  2. Speak clearly into your device
  3. The app will convert your speech to text automatically
  4. Tap the stop button when finished

This feature requires microphone and speech recognition permissions, which the app will request when first used.

What types of appointments can I track?

AnchorPoint includes pre-defined appointment types relevant to dementia care:

  • Doctor
  • Specialist
  • Physiotherapist
  • Occupational Therapist
  • Speech Therapist
  • Neurologist
  • Psychiatrist
  • Psychologist
  • Social Worker
  • Dentist
  • Optometrist
  • Audiologist
  • Dietitian
  • Podiatrist

You can also select "Other" and enter a custom appointment type for any providers not listed.

How do I export my data to share with doctors?

To export your timeline data:

  1. Go to the Timeline tab
  2. Set your desired date range and category filters
  3. Tap the export button (share icon) in the top-right
  4. Choose how to share: email, messages, save to files, etc.

The export creates a text-based report with all your filtered entries, including dates, categories, notes, and mood information. Photos are noted but not included in the text export.

Can I edit or delete entries after creating them?

Yes! You can manage your entries in the Timeline view:

  • To edit: Swipe left on any entry and tap "Edit"
  • To delete: Swipe left on any entry and tap "Delete"

For appointments, you can also tap the "Edit" button directly on each appointment row, or swipe left to delete.

What happens to my data if I delete the app?

If you delete AnchorPoint from your device, all your journal entries, appointments, and photos will be permanently removed. This data cannot be recovered since it's stored locally on your device.

Important: Before deleting the app, make sure to export any important timeline data you want to keep using the export feature in the Timeline tab.

Why won't the app send me notifications?

If notifications aren't working, check these common issues:

  1. Permissions: Go to Settings > AnchorPoint > Notifications and ensure they're enabled
  2. Do Not Disturb: Check if Do Not Disturb mode is blocking notifications
  3. Past appointments: Notifications won't be scheduled for appointments in the past
  4. Device storage: Low storage can sometimes affect notification scheduling

If problems persist, try toggling notifications off and on again in the appointment settings.

How can family members or caregivers help?

Family members and caregivers can assist by:

  • Initial setup: Help set up the app and create the first few entries together
  • Regular check-ins: Encourage daily use and help with entries when needed
  • Appointment management: Assist with entering appointment details and setting up notifications
  • Data review: Use the Timeline export feature to share information with healthcare providers
  • Backup routine: Help establish a regular habit of exporting data as a backup
I'm having technical difficulties. How can I get help?

For technical support, please email us at sorapps.help@gmail.com with:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • App version (found in Information tab)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Any error messages you're seeing

We typically respond within 24-48 hours and will work with you to resolve any issues.

Privacy Policy for AnchorPoint

Last Updated:

AnchorPoint ("us", "we", or "our") operates the AnchorPoint mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information Collection and Use

AnchorPoint is designed as a private, local journaling tool. We do not collect or store your personal data on any external servers. All information you enter into the app, including journal entries, notes, photos, mood logs, and other data, is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Data You Provide

  • Journal Entries: Text notes, category selections, timestamps, and associated mood types.
  • Photos: Images you choose to attach to journal entries from your photo library or taken with the camera. Access to your photo library or camera is only requested when you choose to use these features.
  • Speech-to-Text: If you use the dictation feature, audio is processed by Apple's speech recognition service on-device (or as per Apple's current speech processing policies) to convert your speech into text. We do not store the audio data. The transcribed text is stored as part of your journal entry.

Permissions

  • Camera Access (NSCameraUsageDescription): We request camera access solely to allow you to take photos to add to your journal entries. This access is only active when you choose to take a photo within the app.
  • Microphone Access (NSMicrophoneUsageDescription): We request microphone access solely for the dictation feature, allowing you to speak your journal notes. This access is only active when you explicitly start dictation.
  • Speech Recognition Access (NSSpeechRecognitionUsageDescription): We request access to speech recognition to convert your spoken notes into text for your journal entries. This processing is handled by Apple's services.
  • Photo Library Access (NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription - handled by PhotosPicker): We request access to your photo library to allow you to select existing photos to add to your journal entries.

Data Storage and Security

All data you enter into AnchorPoint is stored locally on your device within the app's sandboxed environment using SwiftData. We do not transmit your journal data to any external servers or third parties. You are in control of your data.

Because data is stored locally, its security is tied to the security of your device (e.g., passcode, Face ID/Touch ID). We recommend you use these security features to protect your device.

Data Sharing and Export

AnchorPoint does not automatically share your data with any third parties. The app includes an export feature that allows you to generate a text-based summary of your journal entries. This export is initiated by you, and you control where the exported file is shared or saved (e.g., via email, messaging apps, or saved to your files).

Data Deletion

You can delete individual journal entries directly within the app. Deleting the AnchorPoint application from your device will also delete all locally stored app data, including all journal entries and associated photos.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our service (though in AnchorPoint, all data is local to the user's device).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
